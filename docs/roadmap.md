# Mathlab Course Roadmap

This roadmap builds toward signal processing while strengthening the underlying math.

## Track 1: Linear Algebra

1. Vectors as coordinates
2. Vector addition and scalar multiplication
3. Dot products as alignment and similarity
4. Vector length and normalization
5. Projections
6. Bases and coordinates
7. Orthogonality
8. Eigenvectors and eigenvalues

## Track 2: Calculus

1. Functions and graphs
2. Rates of change
3. Limits as a motivating idea
4. Derivatives and rules
5. Tangent lines and local linear approximation
6. Optimization
7. Derivatives in signal contexts

## Track 3: Integrals

1. Accumulation as continuous summation
2. Area under a curve
3. Antiderivatives
4. Definite integrals
5. Numerical integration
6. Signal energy as an integral
7. Continuous inner products

## Track 4: Matrix Theory

1. Matrix-vector multiplication
2. Matrices as transformations
3. Matrix multiplication as composition
4. Systems of equations
5. Inverses and rank
6. Determinants as area/volume scaling
7. Eigenvectors and diagonalization

## Track 5: Geometry

1. Coordinate plane review
2. Lines, slopes, and distances
3. Vectors in the plane
4. Angles and dot products
5. Transformations of shapes
6. Curves and parametric motion

## Track 6: Signal Processing Bridge

1. Signals as vectors
2. Dot products as pattern matching
3. Normalized similarity
4. Orthogonal patterns
5. Sine and cosine as basis directions
6. Complex numbers and rotating signals
7. Fourier series intuition
8. Discrete Fourier transform intuition

## Suggested First Path

Start with linear algebra and geometry together:

1. Linear Algebra 01: Vectors and Dot Products
2. Geometry 01: Coordinate Geometry and Vectors
3. Matrix Theory 01: Matrix-Vector Transformations
4. Signal Processing Bridge 01: Signals as Vectors
5. Calculus 01: Functions and Derivatives
6. Integrals 01: Accumulation and Area
